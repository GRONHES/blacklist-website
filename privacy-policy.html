Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Blacklist Mobile ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our game and related services. By playing Blacklist Mobile, you agree to the terms described herein.

1. Information Collected via Epic Online Services (EOS)

Our game utilizes Epic Online Services (EOS) to provide cross-play, matchmaking, and social features. When you log in using an Epic Games account or interact with EOS features, the following may be processed:

  • Epic Account ID: A unique identifier provided by Epic Games to recognize you across sessions.
  • Display Name: Your chosen public name for leaderboards and matchmaking.
  • Online Status: Information regarding whether you are currently active in-game.

2. Technical and Gameplay Data

To maintain a fair and high-performance environment, we process technical data including:

  • Device Information: Hardware model, OS version, and unique device identifiers (for optimization and troubleshooting).
  • Gameplay Statistics: Kill/death ratios, match duration, and progression data.
  • Security Data: To ensure competitive integrity, our systems monitor for unauthorized software or modifications. Fair play is mandatory; any attempt to bypass game mechanics will be logged.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide multiplayer connectivity and matchmaking via EOS.
  • To track in-game progression and rankings.
  • To identify and resolve technical bugs or performance issues on mobile devices.
  • To protect our community from unfair advantages and maintain a secure environment.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. Data is only shared with third parties in the following cases:

  • Epic Games: Data required for EOS functionality is handled according to the Epic Games Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the safety of our players.

5. Your Rights and Data Retention

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your account data. For EOS-related data, these requests can be managed through your Epic Games account settings. We retain gameplay data only as long as necessary to provide game services.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or data handling, please contact the development team through our official developer channels at blacklistmobile.com.
